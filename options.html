Auto-complete
Press Alt + Space to quickly select the first entry in the auto-complete menu.
This also works in the search bar!
Delete Page
Press Ctrl + Delete to quickly delete a page
You can always undo by pressing Ctrl+Z but just be careful
Easily collapse and expand blocks
Press Shift + Scroll Wheel to quickly collapse or expand a block.
Shift + Scroll up to collapse and Shift + Scroll down to expand
Scope Highlighting
Highlight the bullet next to the text area and the sibling directly above it
